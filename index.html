About Us

Company Overview

MONDAN LIMITED, is an enviromentnal Services, Research and Construction firm in Ghana under the Companies code, 1963[Act 179] with it's main office located at Awukugwa Akuapem, close to SDA School. We offer holistic enviromentnal solutions that meet the needs of our Clients [private businesses and governmental organizations] both locally and internationally

Our services include: solid waste management, sanitation, enviromentnal policy and research, enviromentnal impact assessment, enviromentnal permitting, enviromentnal sustainability, circular economy for business consultancies, enviromentnal management systems, occupational health and saftey, and enviromental audit

The firm also provides services from, natural resources management, feasibility studies, land and water contamination assessments and reclamation, training and capacity building, project supervision and management, water resource development and management, and enviromental laboratory analysis services. Moreover, we provide construction services such as design and construction of roads, residential and commercial buildings, water supply systems, and drainage and sanitation systems among others.

VISION

The vision of the company is the be the mdel firm for environmental services, research and construction[ESRC] through provision of high-quality excellent services to clients both locally and internationally
